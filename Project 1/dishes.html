Our Dishes

  1. Butter Paneer Masala

    A beloved North Indian classic, Butter Paneer Masala features soft, succulent cubes of paneer cooked to perfection in a rich, velvety tomato and cream-based sauce. Infused with a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, garam masala, and coriander, and finished with a generous dollop of butter, this dish offers a harmonious balance of mild heat, sweetness, and creaminess. Best enjoyed with naan, roti, or steamed rice, it’s a comforting, indulgent delight that never fails to satisfy.

  2. Pasta

    A delightful Italian staple, this pasta is cooked al dente and tossed in a flavorful sauce made from fresh, high-quality ingredients. Whether it’s a creamy Alfredo, a tangy tomato marinara, or a fragrant herb and garlic olive oil base, every bite bursts with taste. Topped with freshly grated cheese, fresh herbs, or a sprinkle of chili flakes, this dish is a perfect blend of texture and flavor. Ideal as a hearty main course or a comforting meal, it’s a timeless dish that pleases every palate.
